About JobHub SA

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the talent gap in South Africa by connecting skilled professionals with the nation's leading companies. We believe that the right job can transform a person's life and that the right person can transform a business. We are dedicated to creating a simple, efficient, and transparent platform for both job seekers and employers.

Who We Are

JobHub SA was founded in 2025 by a passionate developer Sbonakaliso Mfundo Thabethe and human resource experts who saw a need for a more modern and user friendly job portal in the South African market. Frustrated with overly complex and hardness to find employees/employers, we set out to build something better a hub where opportunity and talent meet seamlessly.
